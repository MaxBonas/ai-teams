## The Opportunity

The AI Team Orchestrator is now **production-hardened** (176 tests, Tier 1-3 complete). However, **knowledge management is still manual**:

- Onboarding: 5 days (should be 2 hours)
- Incident response: 2+ hours (should be 15 min)
- Sprint planning prep: 3 hours (should be 30 min)
- Audit readiness: 8 hours (should be 30 min)
- Decision context: Scattered across Slack/docs (should be searchable)

**Root cause**: Humans doing synthesis work that AI can do faster.

**Solution**: Integrate NotebookLM as **cognitive memory layer**.

---

## What We're Proposing

Add **5 AI-powered notebooks** that synthesize project knowledge:

1. **Architecture & Design** → "How does the system work?"
2. **Operations & Incidents** → "What's broken and why?"
3. **Compliance & Audits** → "Are we compliant? Who approved what?"
4. **Decisions & Learning** → "Why did we make that choice?"
5. **Metrics & Insights** → "How are we doing? What's the risk?"

Team feeds these with:
- Daily automated ingestion (health checks, tests, costs)
- Weekly manual updates (retrospectives, roadmap)
- Real-time decision logging
- Event-triggered incident uploads

NotebookLM synthesizes into instant answers.

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| **Hallucinations** | Use for synthesis only, humans verify before action |
| **Sensitive data exposure** | Screen inputs before upload, never include secrets |
| **External dependency** | Backup with searchable docs; NotebookLM = acceleration layer |
| **Team adoption** | Show early wins, integrate into workflows, measure savings |
| **Data staleness** | Automate daily ingestion, weekly sync, real-time decisions |

**Overall Risk Level**: 🟢 **LOW** (high upside, manageable downside)
